Rice Krispies Treats!

Enjoy Mouth Waterting Dessert in just 15 minutes!

 Directions

Ingredients:

3 tablespoons of butter




1 bag of Kraft Jet Puffed Marshmallows




5 cups of Kellogs Rice Krispies Cereal




Directions:

  1. 1. Heat a medium sized sauce pan to low temperature and add butter.
  2. 2. Add marshmallows to the pan. Stir until melted. Remove from heat
  3. 3. Add the 5 cups of Rice Krispies Cereal to the pan. Stir until well mixed.
  4. 4. Cook for 3 minutes then turn off heat. Cool for 5 minutes and remove from pan.
  5. 5. Cut into desirable portions and serve.
  6. Enjoy!

Bonus: Add your favorite candy snack (chocolates, skittles, etc) for an extra kick!
